# CLAUDE.md

## Vision

A minimalist food diary app that makes food logging effortless through instant photo capture and AI-powered automation, building a personal food journey while enabling social discovery.
Core Value Proposition
"Capture, Track, Share" - Transform food photography from mere social sharing into meaningful personal insights with zero friction.
Key Features

1. Instant Camera Access

Camera-first design: App opens directly to camera view
One-tap capture: Single button to take photo
Quick save: Automatic saving with no required input
Batch mode: Option to capture multiple meals quickly

2. On-Device AI Processing

Automatic food recognition: Identifies dishes and ingredients
Nutrition estimation: Calories, macros, and key nutrients
Meal categorization: Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack auto-tagging
Time & location stamping: Automatic metadata capture
Smart descriptions: AI-generated meal descriptions and tags

3. Personal Dashboard

Visual timeline: Calendar view with photo thumbnails
Eating patterns: Meal timing analysis and trends
Nutritional insights: Weekly/monthly macro breakdowns
Progress tracking: Goals vs. actual consumption
Comparative analytics: How your habits compare to similar users
Meal variety score: Diversity in food choices

4. Social Feed

Privacy-first sharing: Choose what to make public
Follow system: Connect with friends and food enthusiasts
Inspiration feed: Discover meals from the community
Meal reactions: Simple interaction system (not complex comments)
Daily highlights: Featured healthy/interesting meals
Similar users: Find people with comparable eating patterns

## Design Principles

Speed over features: Every interaction optimized for speed
AI does the work: Minimize manual input
Privacy by default: Users control what's shared
Visual-first: Photos are the primary interface
Gentle nudges: Insights without judgment

## Technical Requirements

react-native expo
On-device ML models for food recognition
Cloud sync for backup and social features
Offline-first architecture
Sub-second camera launch time

## Monetization Strategy

Freemium model with basic features free
Premium: Advanced analytics, unlimited history, detailed nutrition data
browse user's food log, No ads in core experience

## Success Metrics

Time to log a meal: < 5 seconds
Daily active users logging at least 2 meals
7-day retention rate > 40%
User-generated content sharing rate > 20%

## MVP Scope

Instant camera with AI food recognition
Personal timeline view
Basic nutritional tracking
Simple social feed
Core statistics dashboard
